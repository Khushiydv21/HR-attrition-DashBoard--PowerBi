# 📊 Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard provides insights into employee turnover by analyzing various HR metrics such as department, salary, tenure, job satisfaction, age group, and employee positions.

The objective of this project is to help HR professionals identify the key factors contributing to employee attrition and support data-driven decision-making for improving employee retention.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Employee Attrition Dashboard
- Salary Distribution Dashboard
- Satisfaction Dashboard
- Tenure Dashboard
- Recommendation Dashboard
- Interactive slicers and filters
- KPI Cards
- Dynamic charts and visualizations
- HR Insights and Recommendations

## 📈 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Employee Attrition Dashboard
Provides an overview of employee attrition across the organization.

**KPIs**
- Attrition Rate
- Attrition Count
- Total Employees

**Visualizations**
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Position
- Attrition by Age Group
- Department Filters
- Age Slicer

---

### 2. Salary Distribution Dashboard

Analyzes employee salaries across departments and positions.

**KPIs**
- Average Salary
- Median Salary
- Minimum Salary
- Maximum Salary

**Visualizations**
- Salary Distribution by Department
- Salary Distribution by Position
- Salary Band Distribution
- Salary vs Attrition Analysis

---

### 3. Satisfaction Dashboard

Measures employee satisfaction and engagement across departments.

**KPIs**
- Active Employee Satisfaction
- Attrition Employee Satisfaction

**Visualizations**
- Department-wise Satisfaction
- Satisfaction vs Attrition
- Satisfaction by Age Group
- Engagement Survey vs Satisfaction

---

### 4. Tenure Dashboard

Examines employee tenure and its relationship with attrition.

**KPIs**
- Average Tenure (Attrition)
- Average Salary (Attrition)
- Average Satisfaction

**Visualizations**
- Attrition by Salary Band
- Attrition by Tenure
- Department-wise Attrition Tree
- Tenure Trend Analysis

---

### 5. Recommendation Dashboard

Summarizes insights and provides actionable HR recommendations.

**Key Insights**
- Production department has the highest employee attrition.
- IT/IS department also experiences relatively high turnover.
- Lower salary bands show higher employee exits.
- Employees with lower tenure are more likely to leave.
- Employee satisfaction plays an important role in retention.

**Recommendations**
- Improve employee engagement programs.
- Review salary structures for lower salary bands.
- Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
- Strengthen onboarding and mentoring programs.
- Offer career development opportunities.
- Introduce work-life balance initiatives.
- Recognize and reward employee performance.

## 📌 Key Business Insights

- Overall employee attrition rate is **33.44%**.
- Production department contributes the highest number of employee exits.
- Medium salary band employees have the highest attrition.
- Employees with shorter tenure are more likely to leave.
- Higher employee satisfaction is associated with lower attrition.
- Executive Office has the highest average salary.
- Salary and employee engagement significantly influence retention.
